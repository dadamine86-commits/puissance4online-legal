About the application

Puissance 4 Online is a mobile application dedicated to the Connect Four game, designed to provide a clear, modern and accessible experience.

Depending on the evolution of the application, available features may include:

  • online matches against other players;
  • a player profile system;
  • a leaderboard and progression tracking system;
  • a match history section;
  • a local play mode on the same device;
  • a training mode or matches against artificial intelligence;
  • settings related to sound, display preferences and user experience;
  • possible integration of advertisements and/or in-app purchases depending on the published version.

Some features may be added, changed, limited or removed at any time in order to improve stability, security, compliance or the overall user experience.
